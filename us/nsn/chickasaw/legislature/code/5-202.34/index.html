Chickasaw Nation Code

Section 5-202.34 OFFICE OF THE CHICKASAW NATION PROSECUTOR.

A. There is hereby created the Office of the Chickasaw Nation Prosecutor within the Chickasaw Nation Executive Department. The office shall be directed by the Chickasaw Nation Prosecutor, who shall be an at-will employee of the Chickasaw Nation Executive Department. The Prosecutor, including all subordinate prosecuting attorneys assigned by the Governor or his delegate to support the Prosecutor's work, must be licensed to practice in the courts of the Chickasaw Nation and be a member of the Chickasaw Bar Association throughout their employment. The Prosecutor, and all assigned subordinate attorneys and employees, shall be subject to all generally applicable employment policies and procedures of the Chickasaw Nation Executive Department.

B. The Prosecutor, and all assigned subordinate attorneys, are hereby empowered to: (1.) represent the Chickasaw Nation in the courts of the Chickasaw Nation with respect to the prosecution of any person accused of violating a criminal provision of the Chickasaw Nation Code or as otherwise may be necessary to carry out and enforce the Chickasaw Nation Deprived Children's Code and Delinquent Children's Code; and (2.) such other functions assigned to the role of "prosecutor" by the Chickasaw Nation Code or the policies and procedures of the Chickasaw Nation Executive Department.

(

(PR41-015, 7/19/2024; PR21-018, 5/21/04; PR38-025, 5/24/2021; PR21-002, 11/21/03.)
