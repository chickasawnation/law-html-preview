Chickasaw Nation Code

Section 17-303.5 EMBEZZLEMENT IN THE FIRST DEGREE.

A. It shall be unlawful to wrongfully or fraudulently appropriate for a person's own use or the use of another any property of another which the person has been entrusted, and the value of such property exceeds one thousand dollars ($1,000).

B. Embezzlement in the First Degree shall be punishable by a fine not to exceed fifteen thousand dollars ($15,000), by a term of imprisonment not to exceed three (3) years, or by both such fine and imprisonment.

(PR39-007, 05/20/2022; PR15-030, 9/23/98; TL14-002, 4/18/97.)
