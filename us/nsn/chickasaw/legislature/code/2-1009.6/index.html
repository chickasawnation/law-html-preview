Chickasaw Nation Code

Section 2-1009.6 OTHER BOARD MEMBERS.

All five members of the board of trustees shall be appointed by the Governor from the Chickasaw Nation, shall serve terms of three years, and shall be confirmed by a majority of the Legislature.

(PR38-020, 5/24/2021; PR22-008, 1/15/05; PR21-025, 9/17/04; PR20-006, 3/21/03; PR14-009, 4/16/97.)
