Chickasaw Nation Code

Section 5-1101.2 DEMANDS FOR EXTRADITION TO THE CHICKASAW NATION.

A. In all cases in which a charge of any offense is brought against a person who cannot be found in Chickasaw Nation, the Chickasaw Nation may demand extradition of such person from the jurisdiction in which such person may be found.

B. When the Chickasaw Nation shall demand the surrender of a fugitive, the actual and necessary expenses to return the person to the Chickasaw Nation may be paid by the Chickasaw Nation.

(PR39-001, 11/19/2021.)
